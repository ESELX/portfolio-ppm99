8th Edition of the
Photography Contest 2017

Honorable mention in the 8th Edition of the Photography Contest 2017 of the Agrupamento de Escolas Pioneiros da Aviação Portuguesa. The theme of this contest was "contrasts", for which I made this photographic record containing the complementary colors blue and orange. The image is inverted horizontally so that the shadows in the puddle gain prominence.
