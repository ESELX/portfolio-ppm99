December 17, 2018

Equality

Inspired by Leonardo da Vinci's paint, La Belle Ferronnière, this consists in a critic of the inequality of genres. The red and pink flowers represent the general thought of society, that this color is "for girls". The blue flowers that are born from the background, refer to an act of criticism of gender inequality and the society that believes in these symbolisms and stereotypes. It is, therefore, a "détournement" of Leonardo da Vinci's work, since I had appropriated it, transforming and giving it a meaning it did not have.
